## ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENT: Each archetype needs a HEARTBEAT SYSTEM that makes them feel like real proactive team members.

For EACH of the 7 roles, add to the design:

### G) Heartbeat (Proactive Monitoring)
- **Cadence:** how often this role checks in (daily, twice daily, weekly)
- **What they monitor:** what data/signals they watch for
- **Trigger conditions:** when do they proactively reach out vs stay quiet
- **3 example proactive messages** this role would send unprompted

### Reference Examples

- **Dev Director (daily):** "I found 3 new grants matching your mission posted this week. The Kresge Foundation one ($75K, due May 1) looks like a strong fit. Want me to draft the LOI?"

- **Marketing Director (twice daily):** "Your newsletter open rate dropped from 42% to 28% this month. I have 3 subject line strategies to test. Should I draft A/B variants?"

- **Finance Director (weekly):** "Monthly financials are ready. Program expenses are 12% over budget but offset by a $15K unplanned gift. Net position is healthy. Board summary attached."

- **Events Director (as needed):** "The annual gala is 6 weeks out. Venue confirmed, but catering is still pending. I've drafted 3 sponsor outreach emails. Priority: lock in the silent auction items."

### Design Principles

The heartbeat should make each director feel like they're ALWAYS working in the background, not just responding when asked. They should:

1. **Surface insights** -- patterns, trends, anomalies the user wouldn't notice on their own
2. **Flag risks** -- upcoming deadlines, budget overruns, compliance gaps, anything that needs attention before it becomes a problem
3. **Celebrate wins** -- donor milestones, campaign successes, program completions, grant awards
4. **Suggest next steps** -- concrete, actionable recommendations, not vague "you should look into this"

### Implementation Notes

- Add this as **Section G** in each archetype's design within ARCHETYPE_DESIGNS.md
- Each role's heartbeat cadence should match the nature of the work (finance = weekly, marketing = twice daily, etc.)
- The trigger conditions should be specific and measurable, not vague
- Example messages should sound like a real human colleague, not a notification system
- Include this section in the updated PRD so it's part of the build spec
