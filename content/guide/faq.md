# Frequently Asked Questions

## About Edify OS

**What exactly is Edify OS?**
Edify OS is a platform that gives nonprofit leaders access to a team of six AI specialists. Instead of hiring for roles you can't afford, you work with AI-powered team members who handle fundraising, marketing, programs, HR, executive support, and events -- all through a chat interface.

**Is this just another chatbot?**
No. A generic chatbot gives generic answers. Your Edify OS team knows your organization, remembers your history, and specializes in their domain. Your Development Director thinks like a fundraiser. Your Programs Director thinks like a program manager. That specificity makes the output actually useful.

**Do I need to be tech-savvy to use this?**
Not at all. If you can write an email, you can use Edify OS. You type what you need in plain language and your team handles the rest.

## Privacy and Security

**Where does my data go?**
Your organization's data -- mission, programs, donor information, documents -- is stored securely and used only to support your team's work within your account. We never share your data with other organizations.

**Who can see my conversations?**
Only authorized users in your Edify OS account. Conversations are private to your organization.

**Can Edify OS access my email or calendar without my permission?**
No. Connecting Gmail or Google Calendar is completely optional. You can use Edify OS without connecting any external accounts. If you do connect them, you control that integration from Settings > Integrations and can disconnect at any time.

**Is my access key secure?**
Yes. Your key is encrypted at rest and in transit. It's never logged or displayed after you save it.

## Accuracy and Limitations

**Can I trust what my team produces?**
Your team produces high-quality first drafts and well-reasoned recommendations. But like any team member, they're not infallible. Always review outputs before sending them externally, especially grant proposals, financial documents, and any communication going to donors.

**What if my team gets something wrong?**
Tell them. "This is wrong -- [correct information]" is all you need. They'll revise. You can also update your Memory with the correct information so future conversations stay accurate.

**Can my team give legal or accounting advice?**
No. Your Development Director can draft budget narratives and flag financial issues in grant proposals, but they're not a CPA. Your HR Coordinator can draft policies, but they're not an employment attorney. Always involve qualified professionals for legal, accounting, and compliance decisions.

**Do they make things up?**
Occasionally, AI tools can produce information that sounds plausible but isn't accurate -- especially for specific facts like grant amounts, organization names, or statistics. Your team is trained to flag when they're uncertain, but always verify specific facts before relying on them.

## Getting the Most Out of Your Team

**How do I get better answers?**
Give your team more context. The more specific you are -- about your audience, your deadline, your constraints, and what "done" looks like -- the better the output. See the **Working With Your Team** guide for more.

**My team's output doesn't sound like us. What do I do?**
Add your brand voice to Memory. Go to Memory, create an entry called "Brand Voice," and describe how your org communicates. Your team will use it automatically.

**I want to use a team member for something not listed in their description. Can I?**
Try it. Your team is flexible. If you're not sure who to ask, your Executive Assistant is a good default -- they can handle a wide range of requests and point you to the right specialist.
