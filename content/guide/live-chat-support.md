# Getting Help with Live Chat

We're here when you need us. Here's how to find support and what to expect.

---

## Where to Find the Chat Button

Look for the **chat bubble icon** in the bottom-right corner of your screen. It's visible on every page in Edify OS.

Click it to open the support chat. You'll see a text input where you can type your question.

---

## What the Support Assistant Can Help With

The support assistant is available immediately, any time. They can help you with:

- **How-to questions** -- "How do I upload a document to Memory?"
- **Navigation** -- "Where do I find the organization profile settings?"
- **Feature questions** -- "Can my Development Director export a grant budget to a spreadsheet?"
- **Billing basics** -- "When does my billing cycle renew?"
- **Troubleshooting common issues** -- Connection problems, formatting questions, slow responses
- **Getting started** -- If you're new and not sure where to begin

For most everyday questions, the support assistant will have an answer right away.

---

## When to Expect a Human Response

Some issues need a real person. Here's when that happens:

**A human team member will follow up if:**
- Your integration (email, calendar, social media) isn't working after troubleshooting
- You report a bug or something that appears broken in the platform
- You have a billing dispute or refund request
- Your question requires access to your account details to diagnose
- The support assistant can't resolve your issue after a few exchanges

When your conversation is escalated, you'll receive a message that says a human team member has been notified. Response times during business hours (9am-6pm ET, Monday-Friday) are typically within a few hours. Outside business hours, you'll hear back the next business day.

**For urgent issues** -- if something is broken and you need it resolved quickly, say "urgent" in your first message. This flags your conversation for faster review.

---

## Chat History and Past Conversations

Every support conversation is saved. To find a past conversation:

1. Click the chat bubble icon to open the support chat
2. Click **Chat History** (top of the support panel)
3. Scroll through your previous conversations by date

You can also search by keyword if you remember what the issue was about.

Past conversations are useful if you need to:
- Reference a solution that was provided before
- Follow up on an open issue
- Share a previous bug report with a new support team member

---

## Other Ways to Get Help

**Help Center:** Browse all articles at any time by clicking **Help** in the main navigation. Articles are organized by topic and updated regularly.

**Email Support:** Reach us at support@edifyos.com for non-urgent questions. We respond within one business day.

**Troubleshooting Guide:** Before contacting support, check the [Troubleshooting](/dashboard/guide/troubleshooting) article -- most common issues have a self-service fix.
