# Organization Setup

## Getting Your Team Ready to Work

Your AI team is only as good as what they know about your organization. Before diving into tasks, spend 15 minutes setting up your org profile. It makes every conversation sharper.

---

## Step 1: Fill In Your Organization Profile

Go to **Settings > Organization Profile** and add:

- **Organization name**
- **Mission statement** (one to three sentences, in your own words)
- **Programs** (brief description of each one you run)
- **Service area** (city, region, or state -- this affects grant research)
- **Budget size** (approximate annual budget -- affects grant eligibility)
- **Population served** (who specifically your work reaches)

This information is stored in your team's Memory and used automatically in every conversation.

---

## Step 2: Build Out Your Memory

Your **Memory** section (in the left sidebar) is your team's knowledge base. Add entries for anything your team should know:

**Good things to add:**
- Your brand voice and tone guidelines
- Key donors and what they care about
- Current programs with outcomes data
- Grant history (funders you've worked with)
- Staff roles and responsibilities
- Any recurring processes your team should know about

The more you add, the more personalized every output becomes.

---

## Step 3: Connect Your Accounts (Optional)

You can connect external accounts to unlock more powerful features:

**Google Calendar** -- lets your Executive Assistant see your actual schedule and schedule meetings directly.

**Gmail** -- lets your team draft email responses and triage your inbox against real emails.

**Social accounts** -- lets your Marketing Director post directly from Edify OS (coming soon).

To connect, go to **Settings > Integrations**.

---

## Step 4: Choose Which Team Members to Activate

You can activate all seven specialists or start with just a few. Go to **Settings > Team** to toggle team members on or off.

If you're just starting out, we recommend activating:
- **Executive Assistant** (for day-to-day tasks)
- **Development Director** (for fundraising)
- **Marketing Director** (for communications)

Add the rest as you need them.

---

## Keeping Your Setup Current

Your org changes. Update your Memory when:

- You launch a new program
- You win or lose a major grant
- Your budget changes significantly
- You hire or lose key staff
- Your strategic priorities shift

A quick update to Memory means your team has current context for every future conversation.

---

## Privacy and Your Data

Everything you add to Edify OS is used only to support your team's work. Your data is never shared with other organizations. See the **FAQ** for full details on how we handle your information.
