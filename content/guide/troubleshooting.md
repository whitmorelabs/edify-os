# Troubleshooting

Running into something unexpected? Here are the most common issues and how to fix them.

---

## "My team member isn't responding"

**What's happening:** The conversation loaded but nothing came back, or the response is taking much longer than usual.

**Try this:**
1. Wait 30 seconds -- some requests (like full grant proposals) take longer to generate.
2. Refresh the page and try again.
3. If the issue persists, check your internet connection.
4. If you're still seeing no response after a refresh, open a new conversation with the same team member and try again.

If a team member is unresponsive across multiple attempts, use the live chat button in the bottom corner to report it.

---

## "The output doesn't match what I asked for"

**What's happening:** Your team member gave you something that missed the mark -- wrong tone, wrong length, wrong focus.

**The fix is almost always clearer instructions.** Try:

- **Be more specific about the task.** "Write a grant proposal" vs. "Write the program narrative section of a grant proposal for the XYZ Foundation, 500 words, focused on our youth mentoring outcomes."
- **State the audience explicitly.** "This is for our board" or "this will go to a first-time donor" changes the output significantly.
- **Say what format you want.** Bullet points? Prose paragraphs? A template? Say so.
- **Paste the source material.** If you're summarizing a report, paste the report. If you're responding to an email, paste the email.

You can also ask for revisions. Tell your team member exactly what to change: "Shorten the second paragraph. Add a call to action at the end. Make the tone warmer."

---

## "I can't connect my email or calendar"

**What's happening:** The connection failed or the integration isn't showing up after authorization.

**Try this:**
1. Go to **Settings > Integrations** and disconnect the account.
2. Wait 30 seconds, then reconnect.
3. When the authorization window opens, make sure you're signed into the correct Google or Microsoft account.
4. Check that you clicked "Allow" (not "Cancel") on the permissions screen.

**If you're using a work or school Google account:** Your organization's IT administrator may have restricted third-party app access. You may need to ask them to whitelist Edify OS, or use a personal Gmail account instead.

**Still not working?** Use the live chat button and share a screenshot of the error message. We'll diagnose it from there.

---

## "The financial numbers seem wrong"

**What's happening:** A budget, projection, or financial calculation doesn't add up or doesn't match your expectations.

**Important:** Your Development Director builds financial structures and frameworks (such as budget narratives and grant budgets) -- they work from the numbers you provide. If the numbers are wrong, it's often because:

- The input data was incomplete or approximate
- A calculation assumption wasn't stated (e.g., fringe rate, overhead percentage)
- A number was misread from a paste or copy

**What to do:**
1. Double-check your input data first.
2. Ask your Development Director to show their work: "Walk me through how you calculated this."
3. Correct any wrong inputs and ask for a revised version.
4. **Always cross-reference financial outputs with your accountant before using them in official documents.**

If you believe the output has a systematic error (not just a data input issue), please report it using the live chat button.

---

## "I want to start over with a team member"

**What's happening:** A conversation went in the wrong direction and you want a clean slate.

**Option 1: Start a new conversation.**
Click "New Chat" at the top of the conversation panel. This starts fresh without the previous context. Your org profile and Memory are still active -- just the conversation history resets.

**Option 2: Redirect the current conversation.**
Sometimes you don't need to start over -- just redirect. Say: "Let's set aside what we were working on. I want to start fresh on this: [new request]." Your team member will follow the new direction.

**Option 3: Clear a specific piece of memory.**
If something incorrect got saved to your org's Memory, go to **Settings > Organization Memory**, find the entry, and edit or delete it.

---

## "How do I cancel or change my plan"

**To change your plan:** Go to **Settings > Billing > Change Plan**. You can upgrade, downgrade, or switch billing periods at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

**To cancel your subscription:** Go to **Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription**. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period. Your data is retained for 30 days after cancellation in case you want to reactivate.

**Questions about billing?** Contact us at support@edifyos.com or use the live chat button.

---

## Something else?

If your issue isn't covered here, use the **live chat button** in the bottom corner. A support assistant is available 24/7 for common questions, and a human support team member can follow up on anything more complex.
